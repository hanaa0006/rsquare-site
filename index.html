About Us

RSQ Construction (Pty) Ltd is a proudly South African company built on more than 35 years of experience in the construction industry. What began in 1998 as an informal service provider has grown into a trusted construction business known for professionalism, quality workmanship, and reliable project delivery.

Based in Cape Town SA, we serve a wide range of clients including residential properties, commercial spaces, educational institutions, and healthcare facilities. Over the years, word-of-mouth recommendations and consistent service excellence has helped shape our reputation and expand our reach.

We specialise in general construction work, property maintenance, ablution upgrades, supply of building materials, irrigation products, beverages, paper goods, and landscaping solutions. As a company that supports local development, we employ skilled South African artisans and use locally manufactured materials wherever possible, promoting the “Proudly South African” ethos.

Our vision is to provide high-quality and efficient services that ensure customer satisfaction while uplifting our communities. We are committed to empowering youth and disadvantaged individuals through skills development and employment opportunities.

At RSQ Construction, we uphold integrity, reliability, and respect in every project. We leave each site in excellent condition and build long-term client relationships based on trust and consistent results.
